About

QrTalk is a fast and secure chat app that connects you with a simple QR code scan. No accounts or complicated setups—just scan, connect, and start chatting instantly. Whether you’re next door or miles apart, QrTalk makes communication effortless.

FAQs

What is QrTalk?

QrTalk is a fast, socket-based chat app that lets you connect and chat instantly by scanning a QR code. No accounts, no hassle—just simple, secure communication.

How does QrTalk work?

QrTalk uses QR codes to establish a direct, real-time connection between two devices. Once connected, you can exchange messages privately without any data being stored or viewed by the server.

Is QrTalk secure?

Yes, QrTalk ensures your conversations are private & secure.

Do I need to create an account to use QrTalk?

No, QrTalk does not require any account creation. You can start chatting instantly by scanning a QR code.

Can I use QrTalk to chat with multiple people?

Currently, QrTalk is designed for one-to-one chats.
